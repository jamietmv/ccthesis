Full Stack Software Engineer

Clothing Cycle is seeking a Full Stack Software Engineer to join our Software Engineering team. Our marketplace has an abundance of one-of-a-kind items from independent artists and crafters. Our challenge is making it easier for buyers to find these remarkable goods, and in turn, support our sellers’ independent businesses.

About the Job

You'll be helping us build new and awesome experiences for people shopping on Clothing Cycle. We're looking for full-stack engineers - you'll be dealing with the MySQL, Memcache, PHP, and front-end stuff (HTML/CSS/JS). The teams working on each feature are small, so there's a lot of meaningful collaboration.

Because we're making user-facing features, we believe strongly in collecting and analyzing numerical data to help guide our decisions. To that end, you'll be able to use our in-house analytics tools to gather information and gain insights.


About You

You’re an engineer who can turn ideas into extremely fast and reliable code that other people will find easy to comprehend and modify. You believe in the connective power of Clothing Cycle’s marketplace, and know how to create the code that will make it stronger.


You will be a good fit if you:

  • Enjoy helping others around you grow as developers and be successful
  • Have excellent written and verbal communication skills
  • Can be autonomous and self-driven
  • Get inspired on a daily basis, think of new ideas and like to share them with others
  • Believe we can always do better to help the environment

Senior Manager, Tax Planning

Clothing Cycle is looking to hire a Senior Manager – Tax Planning and Audits. This new position will support our growth and will be a member of the Clothing Cycle Tax leadership team. The Senior Manager – Tax Planning will work closely with the Tax, Accounting, Finance, and Legal teams to ensure all of Clothing Cycle’s tax planning and audit needs are met.

About the Job

  • Serve as project manager on tax planning initiatives
  • Oversee any tax audits or related inquiries
  • Manage and support Clothing Cycle’s transfer pricing strategy
  • Provide tax research support to the Tax and Accounting teams
  • Address tax questions from various business teams as required

About You

You are passionate about Tax! You are a champion of the strategies and processes that will support a best-in-class tax team.


You will be a good fit if you have:

  • An appropriate tax education, which may be some combination of a CPA or JD and a Masters in Taxation
  • Six to ten years of experience with a background in public accounting and industry
  • Experience with a technology company is a plus
  • A proven track record of success, preferably with accounting firm and technology company experience
  • Ability to work across our international organization and with our external tax advisors
  • A willingness to travel on an infrequent basis/as the job demands
  • A hands-on professional with some supervisory experience is desired

Digital Marketing Specialist

You will be helping to develop and execute Clothing Cycle’s paid search strategy, scaling campaigns for the Clothing Cycle marketplace and integrating with other channels as part of a larger portfolio.

About the Job

  • Lead text ad and PLA campaign management towards acquisition goals, continually optimizing strategy, from keyword expansion to landing page testing
  • Lead mobile paid search strategy, spanning mobile web and app
  • Drive acquisition KPIs, including CTR, conversions, new buyers and sellers, and ROI
  • Manage external agencies and technologies effectively, maximizing service/offerings and recognizing needs for strategic guidance/gaps in internal resources
  • Deliver reporting and insight that accurately and appropriately measures channel performance on a daily through annual basis
  • Partner with marketing team members to develop integrated efforts, such as cross-channel attribution modeling/optimization and unified global campaigns
  • Collaborate with internal, cross-functional stakeholders to strengthen paid search program, such as on-site landing page development and conversion funnel optimization

About You

  • You have 4+ years of online and direct response marketing experience with demonstrated success in managing substantial paid search budget to specific goals and timelines
  • With strong critical thinking and problem solving skills, you couple a data-driven approach and creative thinking with a passion for online marketing, which encompasses up-to-date knowledge of the search industry
  • You have deep familiarity with a bid management tool and/or platform (ex. Marin, Kenshoo, DoubleClick, IgnitionOne)
  • You are highly collaborative and operate with integrity, confidence, and empathy
  • You are passionate about the Clothing Cycle marketplace and deeply care about the member experience
  • Ideally, you have experience in a fast-growth, engineering-driven company, where you’ve learned to be resourceful
