Pupcycle


Fun, yet functional dog coats, sweaters and sweatershirts upcycled from human clothing. I prefer to use on natural fibres such as wool and cotton, but occasionaly I will find an exceptional sweater made out of acrylic (or similar) that I will use.

I am working with an existing garment, sometimes it ends up a little longer or wider to work around pockets or a neat logo. And if you don't see anything in the size you need, let me know and I'll do my best to accomodate.
