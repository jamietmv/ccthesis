A Recurring Dream


A blended harmony of new and reclaimed materials to soothe and intrigue the visual palatte. All of my creations are made incorporating in part, or made entirely, of salvaged materials. I love the challenge of working to make use of every salvaged scrap, and I love doing my part to keep the burden lighter on my local landfill.

My passion is in piecework. I have acquired an amazing stash of scraps through my years of sewing and I am enjoying reacquainting myself with the treasured scraps as I give them new life as lovely, and practical, terry cloth towels.
