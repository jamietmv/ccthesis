Description

This kitsch and comfy cushion is made from an upcycled men's shirt!

The buttons of the shirt act as the fastening for the cushion cover so you can remove the cover and machine wash it.

This cushion is made from soft cotton fabric in a delightful wine and blue stripe - the perfect addition to your sofa, armchair or bed!
