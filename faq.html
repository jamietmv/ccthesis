Frequently asked questions

How much is shipping?

For costumers who wants to send used clothes to us, there is no cost for the boxes and envelopes.

For costumers who is purchasing second-hand clothing and repurposed products from shops, shipping price varies based on the ordered items or individual shop policies. To find out how much shipping is, add the items to your shopping cart and click “Calculate shipping” link under subtotal. Enter your state and postal code/Zip code and press “Calculate” button. You'll see the shipping cost in the shipping column.

How does international standard shipping work?

Currently, we do not ship containers and sencond-hand clothing outside of the United States.

For costumers who is purchasing repurposed products from shops, international shipping methods differ from individual shops. Please visit the shop pages or contact the owners.

What is your return policy

We gladly accepts returns for full refund or exchange within 60 days of original purchase. Refunds will be made in the form of the original payment unless we agree to credit a different payment type. All personalized and final sale items cannot be returned or exchanged.

Can I track my shipment?

You can track your order here. Please enter your order ID which can be found in your order confirmation email, and the email address you used during checkout.
