Description

At a vintage touch to your kitchen with a charming retro print of flowers and fruit!

Set includes:

*2 dish rags each measuring approximately 9 inch squares

*2 pot holders each measuring approximately 8 inch squares

*Machine wash cold / Tumble dry low

The dish rags are made from a high quality terry cloth and a coral Vintage fabric. The terry cloth is bright white and super fluffy! The dish rags are top stitched using a white thread that blends completely on the terry cloth side of the towel.

The two potholders are made using the coordinating vintage material, three layers of cotton batting for extra protection against hot items, and from repurposed denim from recycled jeans.
