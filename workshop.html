Half-Day Hand-Dye Workshop

Mar 1, 2015 | 11:00am-4:00pm

Join us for a fun and relaxed hand dyeing workshop. You are going to give your clothes a whole new look! Bring pieces to work on (aprons, socks, shirts, scraves, etc.) This is a great opportunity to make a personal present for a friend or family member. Just remember — your personal items need to be made of natural materials, like cotton, silk or linen.

We will prepare the dyeing materials. You will learn about different techniques and dyeing materials. After all the measuring and prep work is done it’s just about working with your hands and playing with color!

Sewing Fun for All

Jan 25, 2015 | 12:00-3:00pm

Bring along some clothes or all that extra fabric you’ve been meaning to use. Or, simply grab something here and turn it into something else.

You can start from making a simple coin purse. Jennifer Casper, an inspired artist and maker, will teach you the technique of using sewing machines. After that, you can create your own project. Make a Halloween costume, a cape, a costume, a monster or a miniskirt. Make a stuffed animal or a tea cozy or a shopping bag, a book cover or a tutu. Use your imagination or avail yourself of ours!

Recycled Textiles Weave

Nov 22, 2014 | 1:00-3:00pm

A fun and easy way to learn to weave on a simple pre-threaded loom. Suitable for all ability levels, ages 10 to 100!

Inspired by Japanese ‘Sakiori’ weaving, use strips of cloth and recycled materials to create a table runner, bag or cushion cover. Learn how to weave and make something beautiful – no rules, no mistakes, just creativity!

Basic materials are provided, but you may like to bring your own to experiment with – fabric remnants, an old t shirt, skirt or dress in a pattern or stripe fabric – cotton fabrics are the most suitable for making weave yarn.

Halloween Mask-Making

Oct 18, 2014 | 2:00-3:30pm

Mask-making is an easy way to dress up and express your unique style without making an entire costume. Or if you love costumes, a mask is the perfect way to take your persona to the next level. Fashion a fabulous disguise, just in time for Halloween!

You will start with a mask blank which can be cut into a variety of shapes. Next, we’ll show you how to cover the blank with your choice of fabrics. Then you can unleash your imagination with an assortment of embellishments - feathers, ribbons, appliques, studs, sequins and crystals. All materials will be provided, including scissors and glue - no sewing required! This workshop is ideal for ages 14 and up.

Instructor
Natalie Loew
