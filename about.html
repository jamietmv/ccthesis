Clothing Cycle is the new way to help you recycle clothes. We take your unwanted items, and distribute them to crafters and artists who use fabric to create amazing merchandise.

The main purpose behind Clothing Cycle is getting clothing out of landfills. In the US, approximately 13 million tons of clothing waste is generated each year amounting to about 70 lbs of waste per person per year.

However, the recycling industry reclaims only 15% of the total textile waste. The additional 85% goes straight to landfill. And, the chemicals used in clothing pollute ground water which can be 200 times as toxic as raw sewage.

Let's help reduce clothing waste by supporting the creative use of repurposing. Instead of throwing away the old clothes you used to cherish, send them to us, and we sell them to people who would give those pieces a second chance to shine!


Behind Clothing Cycle

Clothing Cycle is a graphic design Master's thesis project by Yu-Hsuan Jamie Chen.

She is now pursuing the degree at the Academy of Art University. She loves the world of fashion, and most importantly she loves our planet Earth. Like other young people in her generation, she uses clothing to express a part of who she is. With time goes by, she finds herself losing interest in some pieces which she used to love.

She knows if she threw them away in the trash, they go straight to the landfills, and it's harmful for the environment. Where should they go instead? The idea of reusing starts her research. She hopes there is a fun way to not only help the environment but make old clothes shine again.

Therefore, she wants to use graphic design to help clothing waste issue and turn it into a positive cycle of creativity.

Our Goals

Sustainability

We create a perfect cycle of reused clothing. By connecting the dots, we can minimize waste and keep clothing out of the landfills.

Convenience

We simplify the recycling process by providing mailing boxes and envelopes at no cost. You just need to fill them up and drop them at the nearest post office.

Creativity

We promote the creative use of repurposing by helping crafters and artists get the materials they need and providing a marketplace for the products.
