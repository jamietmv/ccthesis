Bohemian Kouture


Bohemian Kouture is all about reusing, recycling, rethinking and reimagining what clothing is about. Rather than buy cheap, disposable clothing whose manufacturing involves exploitation of low-wage workers abroad, I believe in upcycled and giving new life for the materials we already have in such abundance. Making new clothing from discarded clothing that otherwise would end up in a landfill is a passion for me, always changing, growing, expanding!
